Choco Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dark chocolate
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220째C.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
  3. Whisk in the sugar and eggs.
  4. Fold in the flour and vanilla extract.
  5. Pour the batter into greased ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12-14 minutes until the edges are firm but the center is still soft.
  7. Let cool for a minute before serving.

Vanilla Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175째C.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, then the vanilla extract.
  5. Alternately add the flour mixture and milk, beginning and ending with the flour.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased and floured cake pan.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let cool before serving.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175째C.
  2. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the sugar, eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix until smooth, then stir in the boiling water (the batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into greased and floured cake pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let cool before serving.

Strawberry Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup pureed strawberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 175째C.
  2. Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, then the vanilla extract.
  5. Alternately add the flour mixture and milk, beginning and ending with the flour.
  6. Fold in the pureed strawberries.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased and floured cake pan.
  8. Bake for 25-30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let cool before serving.
